Data Scientist

What is a Data Scientist?

A data scientist is a professional who uses scientific methods, processes, algorithms, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data. They combine technical expertise with business acumen to solve complex problems and drive data-driven decision making.

Daily Responsibilities

  • Developing and implementing machine learning models
  • Statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
  • Data preprocessing and feature engineering
  • Creating predictive models and forecasts
  • Communicating insights to stakeholders

A data analyst collects, processes, and performs statistical analyses on large datasets to help organizations make data-driven decisions. They transform raw data into meaningful insights through visualization and reporting.

A data engineer designs and builds systems for collecting, storing, and analyzing data at scale. They develop the architecture that enables data collection and transformation for analysis.
